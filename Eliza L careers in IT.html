Interactive Media Developer


What Is The Job?

An Interactive Media Developer is the person who writes the code behind interactive media. They write, edit and test their code for a variety of things such as computer games, film animation, video and other forms of interactive media. They can also be known as Multimedia Programmers. They can focus on a specific area, such as video games or animations, or have generalized knowledge on various pieces of media. They can work for communication design studios, software development and IT consulting firms, media production companies, interactive software developer teams, large private or public IT organizations, or even freelance and be self-employed.

How Is The Salary? (CAD)

For starting positions, the hourly wage can vary from $19 to $50, with the average being around $32. Median hourly salary ranges between $29 and $56, with an average of $40. For the top makers, the average is $57 an hour, however the range can be from $36 to $84. Now that is quite a large range. The average salary per year is $84,128, or $42.29 per hour. These workers are high in demand, with the outlook being average.
